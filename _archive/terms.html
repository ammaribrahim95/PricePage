Terms of Engagement

Last updated: February 2026. These terms define the professional relationship and operational standards between Pawstrophe Digital and our valued partners.

01/

Scope of Work

Pawstrophe Digital provides specialized digital services including but not limited to:

  • check_circle
    UI/UX Design: Custom prototyping, wireframing, and visual identity systems tailored for digital interfaces.
  • check_circle
    Web Development: Full-stack implementation using modern frameworks, ensuring responsive and accessible experiences.
  • check_circle
    SEO Strategy: Technical on-page optimization, content architecture, and speed optimization for search visibility.
  • check_circle
    Integration Services: WhatsApp routing, Google Maps embedding, contact form setup, and third-party API integration.

All deliverables will be documented in a formal Statement of Work (SOW) prior to project commencement. Any work outside the SOW shall constitute a change request.

02/

Payment Structure

50%

Upfront Commitment

Required to secure project slot and initiate research phase.

50%

Pre-Launch Completion

Due upon UAT approval and prior to live server deployment.

All invoices are payable within 7 business days. Late payments may result in project suspension and a 5% weekly penalty fee. All prices are quoted in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR).

03/

Revisions & Changes

The Man-Day Clause: Any architectural changes or additional features requested outside the approved Statement of Work will be billed at a standard rate of RM 900 per man-day (approximately 8 working hours), depending on complexity.

Each project phase includes two (2) rounds of minor revisions. A "minor revision" is defined as changes that do not alter the fundamental structure, layout, or architecture of the deliverable. Major structural changes post-approval constitute a change request and will be scoped and billed separately.

04/

Delivery Timeline

Estimated delivery timelines are as follows:

Single Page Website 7–10 Working Days
Multi-Page Website 15–20 Working Days

Timelines commence upon receipt of the initial 50% deposit and all required project materials (content, images, branding assets). Delays caused by the Client in providing materials may extend the delivery timeline accordingly.

05/

Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full and final payment, all final deliverables produced by Pawstrophe Digital specifically for the Client shall become the sole property of the Client.

Pawstrophe Digital retains the right to display project assets in our portfolio and marketing materials unless a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) specifies otherwise. Proprietary frameworks, libraries, and tools developed internally by Pawstrophe Digital remain our intellectual property.

06/

Exclusions

The following costs are NOT included in the project fee and are the responsibility of the Client:

language

Domain Registration

dns

Cloud Hosting Fees

api

3rd Party API Fees

Operational costs such as email hosting, SSL certificates (if not free-tier), CDN services, and any ongoing SaaS subscriptions required for project functionality are also excluded.

07/

Support & Maintenance

We provide a 3-month complimentary support period starting from the date of launch. This covers:

  • check Critical bug fixes resulting from original development
  • check Technical errors and compatibility issues
  • check Minor text/content corrections

It does not include new feature requests, major content updates, redesigns, or issues caused by Client tampering with the source code. After the complimentary period, support is available at RM 200–350/month depending on the plan tier.

08/

Subscription Terms

Subscription plans (Single Page RM 280/mo, Multi-Page RM 450/mo) require a minimum commitment of 12 consecutive months. Early termination of subscription plans will incur a buyout fee equivalent to 3 months' subscription value.

Subscription includes: initial design & development, ongoing support, monthly content updates, security patching, and performance monitoring. It does not include domain, hosting, or third-party API costs.

09/

Termination

Either party may terminate the project with 30 days written notice. In the event of termination by the Client:

  • warning Pawstrophe Digital shall be entitled to payment for all work completed up to the date of termination
  • warning A 15% cancellation fee of the remaining contract value applies
  • warning Partial deliverables will be transferred to the Client upon settlement
10/

Limitation of Liability

Pawstrophe Digital's total aggregate liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client for the specific project in question. We shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of profits, data, or business opportunities, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.
