support_agent Support & Maintenance

The complimentary 3-month support covers: critical bug fixes from the original development, technical errors and browser compatibility issues, and minor text/content corrections. It does not include new feature requests, major redesigns, or issues caused by modifying the source code yourself.
Maintenance plans cover core updates, security patches, monthly backups, and performance monitoring. Starter sites: RM 200/month. Growth sites: RM 350/month. This is optional but highly recommended for business-critical websites.
A man-day is approximately 8 working hours. This rate applies to change requests — any work outside the original agreed scope. This includes: adding new features, structural layout changes, new API integrations, or significant design modifications post-launch.
Yes. Emergency after-hours support is available at RM 1,350/man-day (1.5x standard rate). This covers critical issues like site downtime or security breaches outside of regular business hours.

schedule Process & Timeline

Single Page sites typically take 7–10 working days. Multi-Page sites take 15–20 working days. These timelines start once we receive the 50% deposit and all required project materials (content, images, branding assets). Delays in providing materials may extend the timeline.
Each project phase includes 2 rounds of minor revisions. Minor revisions are changes that don't alter the fundamental structure or architecture. Major structural changes after design approval are considered change requests and billed at the man-day rate.
