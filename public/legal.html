01/

Scope of Work

Nexus Studio provides specialized digital services including but not limited to:

    UI/UX Design: Custom prototyping, wireframing, and visual identity systems tailored for digital interfaces.
  • check_circle

  • check_circle

02/

Payment Structure

50%

Upfront Commitment

Required to secure project slot and initiate research phase.

50%

Pre-Launch Completion

Due upon UAT approval and prior to live server deployment.

All invoices are payable within 7 business days. Late payments may result in project suspension and a 5% weekly penalty fee.

03/

Revisions & Changes

The Man-Day Clause

Any architectural changes or additional features requested outside the approved "Statement of Work" will be billed at a standard rate of RM 900 per man-day.

Each project phase includes two (2) rounds of minor revisions. Major structural changes post-approval constitute a change request.

04/

Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full and final payment, all final deliverables produced by Nexus Studio specifically for the Client shall become the sole property of the Client.

Nexus Studio retains the right to display project assets in our portfolio and marketing materials unless a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) specifies otherwise.

05/

06/

Support & Maintenance

We provide a 3-month complimentary support period starting from the date of launch. This covers critical bug fixes and technical errors resulting from the original development. It does not include new feature requests, content updates, or issues caused by Client tampering with the source code.

07/

Termination

Either party may terminate the project with 30 days written notice. In the event of termination by the Client, Nexus Studio shall be entitled to payment for all work completed up to the date of termination, plus a 15% cancellation fee of the remaining contract value.
